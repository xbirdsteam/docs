## 1. Supported Payment Method

- **Bank Transfer via QR Code:**\
  You can easily pay by scanning a QR code and making a bank transfer through your Vietnamese banking app.

## 2. How It Works

1. On the payment screen, select your desired plan or credits package.
2. A QR code will be displayed for your transaction.
3. Open your Vietnamese banking app, scan the QR code, and confirm the transfer.
4. Once the payment is successful, your plan or credits will be activated automatically.

## Notes

Please transfer the exact amount and keep the payment description unchanged. Your payment will be recognized automatically within seconds.

**We will support international payment methods or credit/debit cards in the near future.**
