## What is TestGenAI?

**TestGenAI** is an AI-powered platform that automatically transforms your business requirements, feature requirements, user stories, or project documentation into comprehensive, standardized test cases—ready for your testing process.

With TestGenAI, you simply input your requirements or documents—including user interface (UI) screenshot — and the AI will analyze them to generate detailed test cases. These test cases cover a variety of scenarios, including edge cases, user flows, and business logic validation, ensuring maximum test coverage.

## How it works

TestGenAI makes testing effortless by following four simple steps:

- Save Time: Reduce test case creation time from days to minutes with AI automation.
- Enhance Quality: Ensure your test cases cover all functional flows, edge cases, and business logic.
- No Technical Expertise Needed: Suitable for users of all backgrounds.
- Customizable & Integrates Easily: Export to popular formats and integrate with leading tools.
- Cost Effective: Significantly reduce testing costs compared to manual creation and maintenance.
