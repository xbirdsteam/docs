## 1. Why Plans Matter

Subscription plans determine:

- **The features you can access** (basic vs. advanced)
- **How many <Tooltip tip="">points</Tooltip> you receive or can buy**
- **Usage limits** (e.g. upload files, points per month)
- **Support level**

## 2. What’s Included in Each Plan?

### 1. **Feature Access**

- Higher plans unlock more features

### 2. **Monthly Points Allocation**

- Each plan offers a set number of points per billing cycle—used for generating, updating, and translating test scenarios/cases.
- Unused points may expire or roll over depending on the plan. (No point balance carried over when upgrading apply for TRIAL and STATER 

### 3. **Usage Limits**

- Limits apply to the number of files uploading, points per month, export feature.

### 4. **Support & SLAs**

- Access to support ranges from community help (Free) to dedicated & training (Enterprise).
- Priority support and guaranteed response times are offered on PREMIUM & MAX plans.

## 4. Plan Renewal and Expiry

- Currently, automatic payment and renewal are not supported. You can purchase a plan for 1 month, 6 months, or 12 months. When your plan expires, you’ll need to manually purchase a new one to continue using premium features.
- If your plan expires , your account may be downgraded to a No plan with limited access with AI.
